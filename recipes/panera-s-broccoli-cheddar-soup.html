Panera's Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Christy Denney • 4 servings

0 of 9 steps complete

Quick Follow

Step 1

Met the 1/4 cup butter and sautee the onions on the stove-top until soft. Sprinkle the flour over the mixture. Cook and stir over medium heat for 1-2 minutes.

Ingredients

Optional visual mode for shopping and prep.

1/4 cup butter

1/2 medium chopped onion (chopped)

1/4 cup flour

2 cups half-and-half cream

2 cups low-sodium chicken stock or broth

1/2 lb broccoli ((about 3 cups), chopped into bite size pieces)

1 cup carrot (julienned (can buy matchstick carrots in produce section))

1/8 teaspoon nutmeg ((optional but brings out the flavor))

8 ounces grated sharp cheddar cheese ((2 cups))

salt and pepper

Mise en Place

Steps

Bowl Plan

  • Bowl 1: butter, flour
  • Bowl 2: broccoli, carrot
  • Bowl 3: nutmeg, salt and pepper
  • Bowl 4: medium chopped onion
  • Bowl 5: half-and-half cream, low-sodium chicken stock or broth
  • Bowl 6: grated sharp cheddar cheese
